https://3andman.github.io/263-REPO/project2/start.html 

I love this one, it’s basically the game Balatro but without the money system. In nomal Balatro, you would get money after each bind and spend it on jokers, but here you just get to pick one of 2 jokers after each bind. The loss of the money system and other elements of the game make the game lose a lot of its complexity, but it’s still fun. Balatro is one of my favorite games, so naturally I love this but it’s impossible to play without comparing it to the real Balatro. It makes me just want to go play it.  

I don’t know if I’m playing in the right browser though. It seems like it’s off center for me. I tried it in Firefox and Edge; they looked the same. There are buttons that exceed the screen, so I must scroll to see them. Also, I wasn’t able to see images for the joker power ups; they just weren’t able to load.

https://xp30n.github.io/SAVE-THE-INTERNET/html/Homescreen.html 

	I really liked this as a cool art project. It does a good job at illustrating the quality collapse of the internet and other technologies. It takes the image of Microsoft Windows and shows it bluescreening and demanding payments for performing simple tasks.  It made me think of rumors that Microsoft wants to make Windows 12 a subscription. The degradation of the internet and just using a desktop in general really shows. It makes me nostalgic for Windows XP.
